Jasmin

Jasminum sambac, a popular species in the genus Jasmine is also the National Flower of Indonesia where it is known as Melati, and the Philippines, where it is known as Sampaguita. The Jasmine is a very popular flower around the world especially in the tropics because of its unique fragrance

Rose

A rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa, in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears . There are over three hundred species and thousands of cultivars . They form a group of plants that can be erect shrubs, climbing, or trailing, with stems that are often armed with sharp prickles . Flowers vary in size and shape and are usually large and showy, in colours ranging from white through yellows and reds.

Lotus

Nelumbo nucifera, also known as Indian lotus, sacred lotus[1], bean of India, Egyptian bean or simply lotus, is one of two extant species of aquatic plant in the family Nelumbonaceae. It is often colloquially called a water lily.
